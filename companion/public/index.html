Ready to install

Use the install button when your browser supports it. On iPhone, open this page in Safari, tap Share, then Add to Home Screen.

Step 1

Install on PC

Download EMX Clips, open it once, click Auto Setup Capture, then minimize it to tray.

Step 2

Pair phone

Click Phone Share in EMX Clips. Scan the Vercel QR, then tap Open My PC Clips while both devices are on the same Wi-Fi.

Step 3

Save videos

Preview clips, tap Share, then choose Save Video on iPhone or download/share on Android.

About saving to Photos

Web apps cannot silently write to your Photos app. The clean phone flow is a Share button that opens the OS share sheet, then you tap Save Video. A native iPhone app would be needed for deeper Photos integration.
